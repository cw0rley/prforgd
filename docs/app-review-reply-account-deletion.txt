Hello,

Thank you for the feedback. We have added full in-app account deletion in
version 1.0.1 (build 7), which has been submitted for review.

How it works:

1. Sign in (or create an account) in the app.
2. Go to the "Me" tab.
3. Tap "DELETE ACCOUNT" at the bottom of the screen.
4. A confirmation dialog explains that this permanently deletes the account
   and all synced data and cannot be undone.
5. On confirmation, the account is permanently deleted: any active
   subscription is cancelled, all of the user's data (workouts, favorites,
   equipment settings, subscription records) is removed from our servers,
   and the authentication account itself is deleted. The user is signed out
   immediately and can no longer sign in with the deleted credentials.

This is a true permanent deletion, not a deactivation. The entire flow is
completed inside the app — no website visit, phone call, or email to
customer service is required. Our privacy policy (viewable in the app) has
been updated to describe the in-app deletion option.

A screen recording captured on a physical device demonstrating account
creation, navigating to the deletion option, and the complete deletion flow
from initiation to confirmation has been added to the App Review notes.

Thank you for your time.
