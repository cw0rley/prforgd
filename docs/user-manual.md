# PRForgd User Manual

## What is PRForgd?

PRForgd is a CrossFit workout tracker for Hero WODs, Girl WODs, and Benchmark workouts. It works on your phone, tablet, or computer — add it to your home screen for the best experience.

**Website:** [www.prforgd.com](https://www.prforgd.com)

---

## Getting Started

### Install as an App (Recommended)

**iPhone/iPad:**
1. Open www.prforgd.com in Safari
2. Tap the Share button (square with arrow)
3. Tap "Add to Home Screen"
4. Tap "Add"

**Android:**
1. Open www.prforgd.com in Chrome
2. Tap the three-dot menu
3. Tap "Add to Home Screen" or "Install App"

**Desktop:**
- Just bookmark www.prforgd.com, or in Chrome click the install icon in the address bar

## The Six Tabs

### 1. WODs — Browse Workouts

This is your home screen. It shows all 168 workouts: 130 Hero WODs, 16 Girl WODs, and 16 Benchmarks.

**Search:** Type a WOD name or movement (e.g., "thruster" or "Murph") to find workouts.

**Random:** Tap the shuffle icon to pick a random WOD from your current filtered list.

**Favorites:** Tap the star on any WOD card to favorite it. Tap the "Favs" filter to see only your favorites.

**Filters** (horizontal scrollable chips):
- **Favs** — Only your starred WODs
- **My Gear** — Only WODs you have the equipment for (set up in the Gear tab first)
- **Group:** All / Hero / Girl / Benchmark
- **Branch:** All / Army / Navy / Marines / Air Force / Fire / LEO
- **Type:** All / For Time / Rounds / AMRAP

**WOD Cards show:**
- WOD name and type
- Movements used
- Your PR (if you've logged one)
- Branch badge (color-coded)

Tap a WOD card to see its full details.

---

### 2. Create — Generate or Build Workouts

Two modes, toggled at the top:

#### GENERATE
Creates a random workout using only movements you have equipment for.

1. Make sure you've set your equipment in the Gear tab
2. Tap **GENERATE WOD**
3. Optionally name the WOD
4. Tap **EDIT** to modify the generated workout text
5. Tap **START** (live timer) or **LOG** (manual entry)

Tap **GENERATE ANOTHER** to get a different one.

#### BUILD
Create a fully custom workout from scratch.

1. Enter a name (optional)
2. Pick the type: For Time, AMRAP, Rounds, or EMOM
3. Set the time cap or round count (appears based on type)
4. Type the workout in the text box — or tap **SCAN**

**SCAN Feature:**
Tap SCAN to take a photo of a whiteboard or printed workout. The app reads the text from the image and puts it in the editor so you can review and fix anything before starting.

Tips for scanning:
- Good lighting helps a lot
- Printed text works best
- Hold the camera straight-on
- You can always edit after scanning

---

### 3. Log — Workout History

Shows every workout you've logged, newest first.

**Filters:** ALL / PRs / Rx / Scaled

**Each entry shows:**
- WOD name and date
- Your score (time or rounds + reps)
- Rx or Scaled badge
- PR badge (if applicable)
- Round splits (if recorded)
- Notes (if entered)

**Actions:**
- **Tap** a workout to do it again with the timer
- **Long-press** a workout to delete it

**Export:** Tap the download icon at the top to export your data as CSV (for spreadsheets) or JSON (for backups). Data is copied to your clipboard.

---

### 4. Gear — My Equipment

Select the equipment you have access to. This powers two features:
- **My Gear filter** on the WODs tab (shows only WODs you can do)
- **WOD Generator** on the Create tab (uses only movements you have gear for)

Tap equipment to toggle it on/off. Use **Select All** or **Clear All** for bulk changes.

Your selection saves automatically.

---

### 5. Moves — Exercise Library

Browse all 130+ CrossFit movements with video demos.

**Search:** Type to find a movement by name.

**Filter by category:** All / Barbell / Gymnastic / KB&DB / Bodyweight / Cardio / Other

**Watch demos:** Tap any movement with a play icon to watch a YouTube demo video. Movements without videos show a dash.

Videos also appear on WOD detail pages — tap the movement chips to watch.

---

### 6. Me — Profile & Sync

#### Without an Account
The app works fully offline. All your data is stored on your device.

#### With an Account
Create an account to sync your data across devices (phone, tablet, computer).

**Sign up options:**
- Email + password
- Google
- Apple

**What syncs:**
- All workout results (merged — nothing gets overwritten)
- Favorites (merged — union of both devices)
- Equipment settings

**How to sync:**
1. Sign in on each device
2. Tap **Sync Data** on the Me tab
3. Your workouts from all devices are merged together

Sync never deletes data. If you have workouts on your phone and different ones on your computer, syncing gives you all of them on both devices.

## Doing a Workout

### With the Timer

1. Find a WOD (browse, search, or generate one)
2. Tap **START** on the WOD detail or Create screen
3. The timer screen shows:
   - WOD name and description
   - Rx/Scaled badge
   - Large timer display

**Timer controls (bottom bar):**
- **START** — Begin the timer
- **PAUSE** — Pause mid-workout
- **RESUME** — Continue after pausing
- **RD X DONE** — Record a round split (for rounds-based WODs)
- **FINISH** — Record the final round
- **STOP** — End the workout
- **RESET** — Start over

4. After stopping, enter notes and toggle Rx/Scaled
5. Tap **SAVE**

The screen stays on during your workout so you never lose your timer.

### Manual Log

1. Find a WOD and tap **LOG**
2. Enter the date (defaults to today)
3. Enter your time (minutes:seconds) or rounds + reps for AMRAPs
4. Add notes and set Rx/Scaled
5. Tap **SAVE**

## Personal Records

PRForgd automatically tracks your PRs.

- A PR is only counted when you beat a **previous** result (not your first time)
- Only **Rx** results are eligible for PRs
- For timed workouts: faster time = PR
- For AMRAPs: more rounds (and reps) = PR
- PR badges appear in your history and on WOD detail pages
- The WODs list shows your current PR on each card

---

## WOD Detail Page

Tap any WOD to see:
- Full workout description and movements
- Tappable movement chips with video demos
- Your personal record (highlighted box)
- Complete history of all your attempts
- Rx/Scaled and PR badges on each attempt
- Round splits (if recorded)

**Actions:**
- Tap a movement chip to watch a video demo
- Long-press a past result to delete it
- Tap START or LOG at the bottom

---

## Tips

- **Long-press to delete** — works on history entries and WOD detail results
- **Set your gear first** — makes the My Gear filter and WOD Generator much more useful
- **Scan whiteboards** — use the SCAN button on the Build tab to capture your box's daily WOD
- **Export regularly** — tap the download icon on the Log tab to back up your data
- **Sync across devices** — create an account on the Me tab to keep everything in sync
- **Works offline** — the app works without internet once loaded; sync when you're back online
