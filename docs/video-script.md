# PRForgd — Quick Demo Video Script

**Target length:** 60-90 seconds
**Format:** Screen recording with voiceover
**Device:** Phone (portrait) recommended

---

## INTRO (5 seconds)

*[Show app icon / home screen]*

**VO:** "PRForgd — track your Hero WODs, Girl WODs, and Benchmarks. Here's how it works."

---

## BROWSE WODs (10 seconds)

*[Show WODs tab, scroll through list briefly]*

**VO:** "Browse over 160 workouts. Search by name or movement, filter by type, branch, or equipment you own."

*[Tap the star on a WOD to favorite it, then tap the Favs filter]*

**VO:** "Star your favorites for quick access."

---

## WOD DETAIL (10 seconds)

*[Tap into a WOD like "Murph"]*

**VO:** "See the full workout, tap any movement for a video demo..."

*[Tap a movement chip, show video briefly, close it]*

**VO:** "...and view your PR and past results."

---

## TIMER (15 seconds)

*[Tap START on the WOD detail page]*

**VO:** "Hit start and the live timer keeps your screen on."

*[Show timer running for a few seconds, tap PAUSE]*

**VO:** "Pause, resume, track round splits."

*[Tap STOP, show post-workout fields]*

**VO:** "When you're done, toggle Rx, add notes, and save."

*[Tap SAVE]*

---

## SCAN A WORKOUT (10 seconds)

*[Go to Create tab, tap BUILD, show the SCAN button]*

**VO:** "See a workout on the whiteboard? Tap scan, take a photo..."

*[Take photo of a whiteboard or printed workout]*

**VO:** "...and it reads the text for you. Edit anything, then start."

---

## HISTORY (10 seconds)

*[Go to Log tab, show history list]*

**VO:** "Your full workout history with PRs, Rx badges, and round splits."

*[Tap filter buttons: PRs, Rx]*

**VO:** "Filter by PRs, Rx, or Scaled. Export anytime as CSV."

---

## GEAR & GENERATOR (10 seconds)

*[Go to Gear tab, show equipment toggles]*

**VO:** "Set your equipment..."

*[Go to Create tab, tap GENERATE WOD]*

**VO:** "...and generate random workouts using only what you have."

---

## SYNC (10 seconds)

*[Go to Me tab, show logged-in profile]*

**VO:** "Sign in to sync across all your devices. Phone, tablet, computer — your PRs follow you everywhere."

---

## OUTRO (5 seconds)

*[Show WODs tab with the app looking clean]*

**VO:** "PRForgd. Free at prforgd.com. Add it to your home screen and get after it."

---

## PRODUCTION NOTES

- **Music:** Upbeat, energetic, royalty-free (low volume under VO)
- **Pacing:** Quick cuts, don't linger on any screen more than 3-4 seconds
- **Transitions:** Simple swipe or cut (no fancy effects)
- **Text overlays (optional):**
  - "160+ WODs" when browsing
  - "Video Demos" when playing movement video
  - "Live Timer" during timer section
  - "Scan Workouts" during scan section
  - "Free at prforgd.com" on final frame
- **End card:** App name + URL centered on dark background
